ABOUT US

Frenchkizz started as an association with two artists : Mab (drawings) and myself, Enmiasse (scenario).

Together we created NPC, a webcomic, here on this website.
We went on our way for a while. After a few years, we observed how unjust an author's life typically is here in this country (no social standing or security, poor living conditions).

At the beginning we thought ourselves alone in this endeavour. But as the conventions went on, through talking with amateur authors, we realised something had to be done.

We swiped away our personal website, aiming for something for the community.
All infrastructure is being paid for. And we are going to welcome amateur authors, allowing them through their webcomic pages to properly earn a living, using ads, Utip and such.
